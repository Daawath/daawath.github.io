Welcome to

Daawath is your destination for authentic Telugu cuisine and an exciting fusion of Indian and Chinese flavors. Located in Rajampet, opposite Nayara petrol bunk, we offer a variety of mouthwatering dishes, including aromatic biryanis, flavorful curries, crispy starters, and our signature Mandi.
